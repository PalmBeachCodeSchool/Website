Our Instructors

Paul Needham

Paul is currently Information Technology Director coming to us from a private school in the area and sixteen years of Information Technology experience. He is a Florida native with many family ties to old Stuart. Paul is an FSU certified web developer and spent a portion of his career designing websites for various state agencies in Florida developing the first online stream broadcast of the “State of the State” address during Governor Lawton Chiles’s tenure (March, 1997). While he’s always loved web design and programming he recently learned of the huge demand for good web programmers in the industry and feels he can help others become life-long learners and pursue their dream careers. His passion is running marathons completing 8 major marathons and over 250 races across the country.

Kim Mosher

Kim is a freelance consultant whose interest in computers started in junior high school. By the time he graduated with a BS in Computer Science from New York Institute of Technology he had already worked on projects for several Fortune 500 companies. He enjoys keeping up with the latest technologies and applying them in creative ways to meet his clients’ needs. During the rare moments he’s not in front of a computer, Kim enjoys flying trapeze and teaches classical fencing, although he has no plans to run off and join the circus.

Oleg

Oleg works as a Web Developer for a well-known research institute. He’s a highly personable, pro-active and self-motivated IT specialist with over 15 years of diverse industry experience. He has a passion for learning, continuing education and problem solving and is excited to have the chance to contribute back to the community through teaching.

Roman

Since 2007, Roman has been working as a Web Applications Developer for The PGA of America. His main responsibility was creating and maintaining the online scoring system for major golf events, primarily working with ColdFusion and Microsoft SQL Server. He’s a graduate of Stetson University in DeLand, Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a Bachelor of Arts in Digital Arts, which he attended on a violin scholarship regularly performing with Stetson Symphony and Chamber orchestras. The first five years after college, Roman spent working as a Video Editor as well as doing some multimedia development like authoring interactive CDs and DVDs before transitioning into full-time web application development. Originally from St. Petersburg, Russia, Roman is almost a native Floridian after living here for 20 years. He has many diverse hobbies ranging from photography to Latin dancing, tennis, improvisational comedy, poker and sailing..

Danny

Danny is currently a Marketing Consultant with lots of experience in not only business processes but also internet and social media tools and solutions, with over 20 years of Database development, he has worked for several industries including the Government. Danny began his experience with web development at the University of Arkansas where he began an internet interactive map of his birth country of Guatemala, his work landed in the pages of the now inactive Microsoft Encarta Encyclopedia as its cultural web-source. Danny is blissfully married to his wife Katie and he has 3 boys that he is extremely proud of. Danny also enjoys sports and was a former ranked player in both collegiate and professional circuits.
